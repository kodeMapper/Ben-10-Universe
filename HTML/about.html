A Bit About Me

Hi there! I’m Sarang Gade, the creator of this Ben 10 Universe website. My background in Electronics & Computer Science from Ramdeobaba University Nagpur has always fueled my passion for Web designing. Ben 10 was a huge part of my childhood, and this site is my way of bringing that passion to life. I’ve drawn on my education and experience in Web devlopement to create a space where fans can revisit the adventures of Ben Tennyson and explore the rich world of the Omnitrix. I hope you enjoy the content and find it as exciting as I did creating it!

What Sparked This Project?

I created this Ben 10 Universe website as a tribute to my childhood love for the iconic cartoon series. Growing up, Ben 10 was a major part of my childhood, filled with thrilling adventures and memorable characters. This project is my way of revisiting those cherished memories and sharing the excitement of the Ben 10 universe with other fans. Through this website, I aim to celebrate the series, explore its rich lore, and connect with fellow enthusiasts who share the same passion for the show.
